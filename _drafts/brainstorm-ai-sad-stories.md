- URL: https://kennethreitz.org/essays/2026-03-18-open_source_gave_me_everything_until_i_had_nothing_left_to_give
- Date: March 18, 2026
- Kenneth Reitz (creator of Python's `requests` library) writes about how open source success nearly destroyed him
- Untreated bipolar disorder was masked by a culture that celebrated his manic productivity: "The hypomanic productivity wasn't a warning sign to anyone around me. It was a feature."
- Multiple manic episodes, psychosis, hospitalization, suicidality
- Identity merged with projects — when the projects suffered, he suffered
- A decade later: separated identity from code, prioritizing family and treatment
- Key angle for blog: the human cost of tech culture's "always on" celebration
